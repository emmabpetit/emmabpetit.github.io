Suitcase Redesign

One of the most annoying things for me when I go on vacation is when my suitcases' wheels get stuck and it's hard to maneuver my luggage. This project started as a way for me to re-design the wheels on suitcases and then evolved into an entire project focused on making a new suitcase that addresses the complaints most consumers, including myself, have. First I focused components of the suitcase itself, designing the wheels so they have 360 degree motion and clip in airtight bags that maximize the space in the suitcase. After, I decided to create the concept of an app that would additionally limit the stress of travelers.
