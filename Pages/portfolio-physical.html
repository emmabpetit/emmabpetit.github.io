Album Cover

Music has always been a huge inspiration in my life and I have always loved looking at the album covers of artists because I think it tells a lot about an artist and their music. This painting is an album cover inspired by me that includes personal details from my life. By looking at the painting you get a feel for my style, interests, and personality. Inside the album, the genre of music that would be found is R & B and alternative Pop.

Disney Twisted

This painting was part of a series I did to take popular Disney characters and depict what they would be like in real life without their fairytale endings. My inspiration behind this piece was the growing single use plastic epidemic occurring worldwide, polluting natural landscapes. The structure of the painting was inspired by lenticular printing, which is used to create what some people refer to as “holographic images”. The painting is made from two poster boards and floor quarter rounds connected with hot glue and a cardboard stabilizer.
